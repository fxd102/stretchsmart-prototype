Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

StretchSmart is operated by Aviatrix Adventures LLC. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Onboarding preferences: Your selected health concerns, age range, and gender (all optional) — used to personalize your exercises and insights.
  • Exercise activity: Which exercises you complete, your feedback (easy/challenging/couldn't complete), and session duration — used to adapt difficulty and progression.
  • Nudge settings: Your preferred number of daily reminders and timing preferences.
  • Usage patterns: Days active, streak data, and exercise history — used to power features like the "Days you've moved" counter.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number (unless you create an account).
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or files.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Where Your Data Lives

Your exercise data and preferences are stored on your device. If you create an account to sync across devices, your data is stored on our servers with encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which exercises are most popular, average session length) to improve the app. This data cannot identify you individually.

Third-Party Services

StretchSmart may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple HealthKit / Google Fit: Only if you explicitly opt in. Used to log exercise minutes. We read nothing from these services — we only write.
  • Push notifications: Delivered through Apple Push Notification Service or Google Firebase Cloud Messaging. No personal data is included in notification payloads.

Your Rights

  • You can delete all your data at any time from within the app (Settings > Delete My Data).
  • You can use the app without creating an account.
  • You can opt out of analytics in the app settings.
  • If you're in the EU, you have rights under GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.
  • If you're in California, you have rights under CCPA including the right to know, delete, and opt out of data sales (we don't sell data).

Children's Privacy

StretchSmart is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
