AviatrixAdventures.com

Domain: Owned • Status: Vision phase

A passion-first aviation site. Flying stories, culture, the romance of it — not career-track advice. Promotes women in aviation but all-inclusive. The voice comes from experience: commercial pilot (single engine land), founded a flight school from scratch in 2012, flew competition aerobatics. Left aviation in 2021. Coming back.

The Big Dream

Circumnavigate the United States. Every stop is a story: the airports, the people, the weather calls, the small-town diners under the pattern. The trip IS the brand. Write it as you fly it.

This isn't a blog idea — it's a book and a brand. A commercial pilot with comp aero experience writing from the road has natural credibility and a rare perspective.

Launch Plan

The site doesn't need to wait for the trip. It can build toward it:

  1. Now: Landing page — who you are, what the trip is, newsletter signup. Simple, clean, the name does the work.
  2. Warm-up content: Stories from the flight school days. Competition aero experiences. Why you stopped. Why you're going back up. The "bad chapter to dream trip" arc is genuinely compelling.
  3. The trip: Content writes itself. Each leg is a post. Photos, route maps, the human moments. Newsletter goes out from wherever you land.
  4. After: Book, speaking, community — or just a beautiful archive. No pressure to monetize if it doesn't feel right.

Monetization (Optional)

Referral links (headsets, flight bags, training courses) are possible but can make a site feel generic. The real asset is the stories and perspective. Options to explore:

  • Newsletter with built-in audience (monetize later)
  • Curated gear recs — "what I actually use," not affiliate spam
  • Sponsored content from aviation brands (once audience exists)
  • Book from the circumnavigation stories

Decision: not yet. Build the content first, monetize what works.
