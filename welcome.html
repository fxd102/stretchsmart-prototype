Your body remembers.

Even when you forget.

At 80, the people who can still pick up their grandkids off the floor have one thing in common: they started moving intentionally in their 40s and 50s.

You just took the first step.

Source: Longitudinal studies on functional independence in aging (Pahor et al., JAMA 2014; Attia, Outlive, 2023)

DESIGN NOTES

Flow: Onboarding quiz (90s) → this welcome screen (30s) → first exercise (60s) → celebration.

Goal: Under 3 minutes from install to first exercise completed. Every screen between download and Day 1 is a dropout point.

Personalization: The hook text above adapts based on onboarding picks. Someone who chose "desk work recovery" gets a different insight than someone who chose "stay independent as I age."

Inspiration: Reframe's Day 1 feels like a revelation, not a chore. Peter Attia's longevity urgency without the fear. The exercise is almost an afterthought — the insight is the gift.

The Anti-Metric: "Days you've moved: 1" appears here for the first time. It only ever goes up.
