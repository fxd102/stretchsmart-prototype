## Idea #3: AviatrixAdventures.com
- Domain: aviatrixadventures.com
- **Faith is a commercial pilot (single engine land).** Started a flight school from scratch in 2012. Flew competition aerobatics. Left aviation 2021 due to difficult life events.
- **Vision:** Passion-first aviation site — flying stories, culture, promotes women in aviation but all-inclusive. Not career-track content.
- **The big dream:** Circumnavigate the US and write the stories en route. The trip IS the brand.
- **Launch plan:** Landing page now → warm-up content (flight school stories, comp aero, the comeback arc) → trip content writes itself → book/archive after
- **Monetization:** Undecided. Open to referral links but not committed. Content first.
- **Color scheme direction:** Rosie the Riveter feel — navy blue, warm bandana red, cream/khaki, muted gold accent. Vintage-strong, not over the top. (Faith's pick, Mar 24)
- **Page on prototype site:** `/aviatrix` (built by #503, Mar 24 2026)
